The Craft

A trade you can't rush, done by someone who won't.

Reupholstery isn't slipcovering. It's stripping a piece back to its bones — pulling tacks, retying springs, replacing rotted foam and burlap — then rebuilding it in material that outlasts the original. That takes a steady hand and a lot of patience. At Frederic's, that's the whole point.

Whether it's the bench seat out of a '72 pickup, the captain's chairs off a bass boat, or your grandmother's wingback, the work is measured, cut, and sewn in-house in Gonzales. Nothing gets shipped off. Nothing gets rushed. And it shows in a finish that a 5.0-star, word-of-mouth reputation was built on.

What We Cover

Four disciplines. One bench.

Most shops pick a lane. Frederic's runs the whole road — automotive, marine, furniture, and commercial — which means the same trained eye that re-skins a sports seat also knows how to make a restaurant booth survive a decade of service.

01

Custom automotive interiors

Seat covers and full re-skins, door panels, headliners, carpet, convertible tops, and classic-car restorations. Factory-correct or fully custom — two-tone leather, diamond stitch, perforation, piping. Cars, trucks, and hot rods welcome.

  • Seat re-skins
  • Headliners
  • Convertible tops
  • Classic restoration
02

Boat & marine upholstery

Louisiana runs on water, and salt and sun are hard on a boat. We rebuild helm and bench seating, jump seats, casting decks, and cushions in UV- and mildew-resistant marine vinyl with rot-proof foam and stainless fasteners — built to take the lake and the bay.

  • Helm & bench seats
  • Casting-deck cushions
  • Marine vinyl
  • Snap covers
03

Furniture reupholstery

Sofas, wingbacks, dining chairs, ottomans, headboards, antiques and heirlooms — frames retied, foam and padding renewed, and re-covered in your fabric or leather. The kind of piece that's built better than anything on a showroom floor deserves to be saved. We help you save it.

  • Sofas & chairs
  • Antiques
  • Headboards
  • Custom cushions
04

Commercial & contract

Restaurant booths and banquettes, bar stools, waiting-room and office seating, medical and salon chairs. Commercial-grade vinyl and fabrics that hold up to daily traffic and cleaning — turned around with as little downtime for your business as possible.

  • Booths & banquettes
  • Bar & office seating
  • Contract vinyl
  • Fast turnaround

How It Works

From worn out to worth showing off.

  1. 01

    Quote

    Tell us the piece and what you want. Send a couple photos or bring it by — we'll talk material and give you a straight, honest estimate.

  2. 02

    Teardown

    The piece comes apart to the frame. We inspect the springs, foam, and structure, and fix what's failing underneath — not just what you can see.

  3. 03

    Rebuild

    New foam, padding, and support go in. Patterns are cut and sewn in-house to fit your piece exactly, in the material you chose.

  4. 04

    Finish

    Everything's fit, stretched, and detailed by hand — tight seams, clean welting, no loose ends. You get back something built to last, not just re-covered.
